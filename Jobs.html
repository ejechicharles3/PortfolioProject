Hi, here are some of my notable applications built with the technologies listed in about and home page. The first project is built to solve the challenges faced by Supermarkets or Malls and Pharmacies. What is this problem? A lot of Supermarkets face huge loss due to inefficient way of tracking products before expiring. This application known as ExpireTracker tracks stocks, check their expiring dates and notifies the store Manager and/or MD about the list of products that would expire.
It keeps track of staff, Suppliers, payments, reciepts, stock balance, transaction history, etc. It is open for Modifications, with this MD/CEO's do not need to worry anymore about their products.


The second application is developed purely for Resume and/or portfolio. It was developed with HTML and CSS, and it's a free template for anyone who is interested in building a unique Resume or Portfolio. You can get it for free in my Github repository, all you need do is follow me on Github.
Note: You can modify it to suite your choice of design of Resume. Thanks for checking on me.
